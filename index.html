Privacy Policy for NINJUMP

Last updated: 24 May 2026

Thank you for playing NINJUMP. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and protected when you use the game.

By installing or playing NINJUMP, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Age Requirement

NINJUMP is intended for users aged 13 and over. The game does not contain any content that is unsuitable for younger audiences, but it is not designed for children.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe that a child under 13 has provided personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Automatically Collected Information

When you play NINJUMP, certain non-personal information may be collected automatically, such as:

  • Device type, model, and operating system
  • Gameplay data (e.g., score, progress, session length)
  • Crash reports and performance data
  • Approximate location (country/region only)
  • Advertising identifiers (such as Google Advertising ID)

This information does not identify you personally.

3. Advertising and Third-Party Services

NINJUMP uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect information such as:

  • Advertising ID
  • IP address
  • Device information
  • Interaction with ads (views, clicks, etc.)

This information is used for:

  • Delivering ads
  • Preventing fraud
  • Measuring ad performance
  • Personalising ads (if allowed by your device settings)

You can disable personalised ads at any time in your device's Google settings.

AdMob's data practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve gameplay
  • Fix bugs and crashes
  • Display ads
  • Ensure the game works correctly on your device
  • Comply with legal and platform requirements

We do not sell your personal information.

5. Children's Privacy

Although NINJUMP is intended for users aged 13+, the game contains no inappropriate content. To protect younger users who may still access the game:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children
  • Ads shown to younger users are handled according to Google's child-appropriate ad policies
  • Personalised ads may be limited or disabled automatically based on device or platform settings

If you believe we have collected personal information from a child under 13, please contact us.

6. Data Sharing

We may share non-personal information with:

  • Google AdMob (for ads)
  • Analytics or crash reporting services (if used in future updates)

We do not share personal information with any other third parties.

7. Data Retention

We retain non-personal data only as long as necessary to:

  • Operate the game
  • Improve performance
  • Meet legal or platform requirements

Third-party services such as AdMob may have their own retention policies.

8. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

9. Your Choices

You can:

  • Disable personalised ads in your device's Google settings
  • Reset your advertising ID
  • Uninstall the game to stop all data collection

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted at the same URL where this policy is provided.
